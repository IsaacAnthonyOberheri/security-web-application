FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Please reach us at support@securetechsolutions.org if you cannot find an answer to your question.

What cybersecurity services do you offer?
    We provide a wide range of cybersecurity services to protect your business from cyber threats. Our offerings include:
  • Network Security: Protecting your network infrastructure from attacks.
  • Endpoint Protection: Safeguarding devices such as computers, smartphones, and tablets.
  • Security Audits & Assessments: Identifying vulnerabilities in your systems through detailed security assessments.
  • Incident Response: Quickly addressing and mitigating cyber incidents and breaches.
  • Managed Security Services (MSS): 24/7 monitoring and management of your security systems.
  • Compliance & Risk Management: Helping you meet industry standards and reduce security risks.
How do you protect my business from cyber threats?
    Our approach is multi-layered, using advanced tools and strategies to protect your data, network, and devices. This includes:
  • Threat Intelligence: Monitoring emerging cyber threats to proactively defend your assets.
  • Firewalls & Encryption: Implementing strong firewalls and encrypting sensitive data.
  • User Awareness Training: Educating your employees about phishing, social engineering, and best security practices.
  • Real-Time Monitoring: Continuously monitoring your network to detect any suspicious activity or breaches.
  • Patch Management: Ensuring all software and systems are up-to-date to minimize vulnerabilities.
Do I need cybersecurity services if I already have antivirus software?
    While antivirus software is an essential part of your security strategy, it’s only one piece of the puzzle. Cybersecurity encompasses more than just virus protection. Our services provide a comprehensive defense strategy that includes:
  • Protection against advanced threats like ransomware, phishing, and insider attacks.
  • Continuous monitoring of your network and devices to detect and respond to real-time threats.
  • Regular security audits to identify and fix vulnerabilities that antivirus software may miss.
  • Compliance with industry regulations (e.g., GDPR, HIPAA) to ensure your business stays secure and legally compliant.
